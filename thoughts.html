Career October 2024

My Co-op Experience at AtriCure: From Data Pipelines to Documentation

3 min read

Working as an Application and Data Analyst at AtriCure has been an incredible learning experience. Over the past year, I've had the opportunity to work with Azure ETL data pipelines, PowerApps, and PowerBI, while also maintaining extensive project documentation for cross-functional teams.

One of the most valuable lessons I learned was the importance of clear documentation. When you're working with 13+ data pipelines and multiple team members, having well-organized documentation isn't just helpful, it's essential. Through this role, I developed skills in data integration, business intelligence, and technical communication that will serve me throughout my career.

Building a Data Visualization Dashboard for a Local Boba Shop

4 min read

When Milk Jar Franchise approached me to help visualize their sales data, I knew this would be a great opportunity to apply my data analytics skills to a real-world business problem. Learning how to use PowerBI through self-teaching, I managed to create a simple interactive dashboard that helped them understand their sales patterns and make data-driven decisions about inventory and staffing.

Reflection July 2024

Redesigning UC's Volunteering Platform: A UX Journey

4 min read

Creating 40+ prototypes in Figma taught me that great design is about iteration. Each round of feedback brought us closer to a solution that truly served the students. This project showed me the importance of user research and the value of listening to the people who will actually use your product. Even though the project never launched, the experience was invaluable in shaping my approach to UX design.

Teaching 200+ Students: Lessons from Being a Peer Leader

3 min read

This experience taught me the importance of patience, clear communication, and adaptability. I learned how to explain complex concepts in simple terms and to be responsive to the diverse needs of students.
